Famous Pakistani Dishes

Pakistan is known for its rich and flavorful cuisine. Every province has its own unique dishes and cooking style.

  • Biryani
  • Nihari
  • Karahi
  • Chapli Kebab
  • Haleem
  • Sajji

Whether it's spicy street food or traditional family recipes, Pakistani food is loved around the world for its delicious taste.

Biryani

A delicious rice dish prepared with spices, meat, and potatoes.

Nihari

A slow-cooked meat curry traditionally served with naan.

Karahi

One of Pakistan's most famous chicken and mutton dishes.

Chapli Kebab

A spicy minced meat kebab originating from Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
